    B.TECH

    A Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program is a dynamic undergraduate experience characterized by its specialized tracks, practical learning approach, and industry-centric curriculum. Students can choose from a range of specializations such as Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering, tailoring their education to their career aspirations. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning through laboratory work, projects, and internships, ensuring that graduates possess both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. B.Tech programs often incorporate innovative projects and research opportunities
